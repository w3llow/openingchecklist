Opening Checklist

  • Unlock the Front Door

  • Turn on overhead heaters in the Yoga Room
  • Prep chilled towels, replenish all towel stations
  • Turn on lobby and bathroom music
  • Turn on computer, log in to Mariana Tek, Slack, & Zendesk

  • Tidy and refill amenities (where necessary)
